Tax registrations

If you already have an ABN, you can apply here for GST and other tax registrations. Note that you will need to have either an AUSkey or myGov login to use this service. If you don’t have an ABN, go back to the home page to start an application. You can also register for tax registrations during your ABN application.

Click on the links below to learn more about each item. If you are ready to start the registration, login below:

  • Goods and services tax (GST)
  • Pay as you go withholding (PAYG)
  • Fringe benefits tax (FBT)
  • Luxury car tax (LCT)
  • Fuel tax credits (FTC)
  • Wine equalisation tax (WET)
